Main product
Applanation
Tonometer
Made in
Spain

The applanation tonometer is the gold-standard instrument for measuring intraocular pressure (IOP). Based on the Imbert-Fick principle, it flattens the cornea with a known force and measures the required pressure, delivering the most reliable data for glaucoma diagnosis.

Our model is designed to integrate seamlessly with the most common slit-lamp platforms on the market, offering straightforward installation and ergonomics carefully considered for the clinician. Available in two configurations: slit-lamp mounted and standalone.
